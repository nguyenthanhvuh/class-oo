Writing

Chapter 5 addresses writing. I want you to find a reasonable web page that nonetheless could be improved by following Krug's guidelines for rewriting it. You should produce two artifacts:

  1. A screenshot of the original page.
  2. A rewritten (and improved) version of that original page.
You also need a commentary that explains the changes. This can be a separate document or some form of annotation on the two prior artifacts. Be clear about what changes were made, and why the changes implement specific guidelines from Krug.

Note that you may also need to use some of Krug's other guidelines for writing from Chapter 3 (formatting, headers, bullets, etc.) Use those as appropriate.

Post your solution on Piazza. Be prepared to share your example with the class.
