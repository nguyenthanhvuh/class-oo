A Norman Door is a door with inconsistent or missing signifiers for an (intended) affordance.

Find a Norman Door somewhere in pandemic land. Document your find (with a short video or some still shots). Explain what the intended affordance is, and explain the signifier problem with that affordance.

Doors can have all kinds of usability problems; not all problematic doors are Norman Doors. Find a door with a usability problem that is not a "Norman Door" problem. Document your find by explaining the usability problem. In particular, explain why it's not a Norman Door.

Post your result to Piazza under an "Assignment 1 Solutions" thread. (If there isn't one - make one!)

Be prepared to explain your solution to the class. We'll fire up the Randomizer and enjoy some presentations.
