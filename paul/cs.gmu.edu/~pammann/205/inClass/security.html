SWE 205 In-Class Exercise
Spring 2020

Work with your group.

In this exercise I want you to find two examples relating usability and security. These can be cyber-security, but they can also be physical security. Ideally, these examples are from personal experience, but that's not required.

One example should follow the argument in the slides: decreased usability leads to decreased security. (Or, you can flip it around as increased usability leads to increased security.)

The other example should follow the reverse argument: decreasing usability can sometimes increase security. In some ways, this is a much more interesting exercise, because it's not usability in general that's lost, but rather some very specific aspect of it.

Be prepared to present your examples to class.
