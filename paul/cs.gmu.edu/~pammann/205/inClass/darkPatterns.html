Work with your group

The point of this exercise explore dark patterns. From your reading, you know that they are very common. To make this more interesting, we will focus on dark patterns that are less common, but still deemed serious - as determined by the votes in the reading reflection:

  • Privacy Zuckering
  • Roach Motel
  • Forced Continuity
  • Friend Spamming
Find examples from this list that you think are especially noteworthy and be prepared to share with the class.

Restriction: Pick web sites where usability is decent. Avoid obviously malicious sites.
