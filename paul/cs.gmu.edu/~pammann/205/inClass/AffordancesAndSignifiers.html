SWE 205 In-Class Exercise
Affordances and Signifiers

Work with your group (as identified on Piazza).

For some of the objects below:

  • Identify affordances, intended or otherwise.
  • Identify signifiers for the intended affordances. (Note that unintended affordances cannot, by definition, have associated signifiers.)
  • Often, information for how to access an object's affordances is not visible. That is, signifiers may be deliberately left out. If appropriate, identify this situation. How is this situation typically dealt with?
Possible objects
  • Sunglasses.
  • An (old-school) metal house key.
  • A link in a desktop browser window.
  • An icon on a phone screen.
  • One or more objects chosen by your group. (I'd like to see at least one of these.)
