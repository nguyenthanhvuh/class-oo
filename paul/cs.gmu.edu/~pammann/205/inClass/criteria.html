SWE 205 In-Class Exercise
Schneiderman's 5 Criteria

Names:

This is an in-class group exercise. Do this with your group.

  1. Pick a specific everyday object such as a bike, a dishwasher, or a needs-to-be-assembled (e.g. IKEA) piece of furniture.
  2. Evaluate the interface with Schneiderman's 5 criteria.
  3. Which criteria are most important? Why?
  4. How does the object score on these criteria? Why?
