SWE 205 In-Class Exercise
Spring 2021

Work with your group.

In Chapter 4, Krug distinguishes mindless clicks from difficult choices. In the reading reflection, most students thought that the latter were often (but not always) more a result of poor design than inherent complexity. Let's see that in action!

Find a (reasonable) web page with:

  • What you believe is a difficult choice
  • That could be made less difficult with better design
Be prepared to explain why you think the choice is difficult and why you think the redesign alleviates the difficulty. If you have time, do a quick mock-up to illustrate your redesign.

We'll synch up at 2:15 and see what the various groups have come up with.
