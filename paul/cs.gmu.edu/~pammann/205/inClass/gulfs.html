SWE 205 In-Class Exercise
Gulfs of Execution and Evaluation

Do this with everyone in your group.

  1. Consider three everyday kitchen objects: An electric stove; a gas stove; and a microwave oven.

    For each object, what is the gulf of execution? Specifically, what is the goal, and what is the action needed to achieve that goal?

    What does the user have to learn to use the object? Specifically, what mental model does the user have to construct? How does the user learn this?

    For each object, what is the gulf of evaluation? Specifically, how does the object inform the user about progress towards their goal?

  2. Repeat the exercise with a sports watch, Fitbit, or other personal monitoring device.
